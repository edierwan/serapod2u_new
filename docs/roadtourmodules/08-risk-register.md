## Risk summary

Highest-priority risks to address before first real campaign:
1. tenant isolation in RLS
2. survey persistence alignment
3. settings source-of-truth mismatch
4. analytics correctness
5. production DB drift on reward logic

The rest of the risk profile is still meaningful, but those five are the current blockers most likely to damage either data integrity or operator trust.
